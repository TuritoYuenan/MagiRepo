Plots and Contents

Genres (According to Wikipedia)

Both are Magical girl animes
Sailor Moon does not have any extra genres Madoka Magica have Dark Fantasy and Psychological Thriller genres additionally
Both are not Yuri animes de jure, but de facto
Meaning that both are not advertised to have yuri contents, but involves them minorly.
Yuri elements in Sailor Moon only appears since season S and minorly affects the main plot MadoMagi's plot depends heavier on yuri elements which appears platonic and turns to romantic in the Rebellion movie

Ideology

Both have a major impact on the Magical girl genre
Sailor Moon is often considered the most iconic Magical girl anime as it had set off a new era of the genre MadoMagi is famous for its dark twist that shook the world around.
The anime does not necessarily deconstruct the Magical girl genre, but does influence later generations
